Fashion Rewind: Y2K

Y2K comeback? That's a yes! Explore the lastest fashion trends as we rewind back to the early 2000's.

Flared jeans

Flared jeans are in as skinny jeans are out. Wide-legged jeans that are low rise have taken back their spot over the famous high-rise skinny jeans. Flared jeans pair very well with boots and are also stylish with sneakers.

Cargo pants

Everyone is reaching for cargo pants nowadays as they are comfy, yet stylish. The urban chic look is super popular and the huge pockets are a big hit.

Velour Tracksuits

Rock a velour tracksuit like Paris Hilton. Cute and extremely soft! Comfort is everything and velour tracksuits are a showstopper outfit offering a velvety smooth feeling.

Baguette bags

Baguette bags have come back into fashion. They are a small and cute bag to carry around. Their compact size add a nice finishing touch to any outfit and can complement any day or night fit.
